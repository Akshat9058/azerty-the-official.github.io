Qwerty

Qwerty Is A Keyboard Design For Latin-Script Alphabets.

The Name Comes From The Order Of The First Six Keys On The Top Left Letter Row Of The Keyboard (Q.W.E.R.T.Y).

The Qwerty Design Is Based On A Layout Created For The Sholes And Glidden Typewriter And Sold To E.

Remington And Sons In 1873.
